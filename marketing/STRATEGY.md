# PriceMate Australia — Marketing Strategy (0 → Launch)

## Positioning

**Don't say:** "Price comparison app"  
**Say:** "AI Shopping Agent that finds the true lowest price — including coupons, student discounts, cashback & shipping."

### Target Audience (Phase 1)

1. **Australian university students** — highest intent, student mode is a killer feature
2. **Deal hunters** — people already using Honey, ShopBack, Cashrewards separately
3. **International students in Adelaide/Sydney/Melbourne** — new arrivals need laptops, SIMs, furniture

## Launch Phases

### Phase 0: Pre-Launch (Week 1-2)

| Action | Cost | Goal |
|--------|------|------|
| Deploy to Vercel (free) | $0 | Live URL to share |
| Create TikTok + Instagram accounts | $0 | @pricemate.au |
| Post 3 demo videos (screen recordings) | $0 | "I built an app that finds true prices" |
| Share in r/australia, r/Adelaide, r/unimelb | $0 | First 100 users |
| Post in uni Facebook groups | $0 | Student acquisition |

### Phase 1: Student-First Growth (Month 1)

**Hook:** "Turn on Student Mode — see what you actually pay as a uni student."

| Channel | Tactic |
|---------|--------|
| TikTok | "POV: you find out JB Hi-Fi has a hidden student discount" |
| Instagram Reels | Before/after True Price comparisons |
| Uni groups | "Free app I made for students shopping for laptops" |
| Word of mouth | Ask 10 friends to try + share |

**Goal:** 500 users, 50 daily searches

### Phase 2: Content SEO (Month 2-3)

Write comparison pages (add to site later):

- "Cheapest iPhone 17 Pro in Australia 2026"
- "Best student laptop Australia under $1500"
- "JB Hi-Fi vs Harvey Norman — who is cheaper?"

These rank on Google and bring organic traffic forever.

**Goal:** 2,000 users, first affiliate revenue

### Phase 3: Monetization (Month 3+)

| Revenue Stream | How |
|----------------|-----|
| Affiliate links | Amazon Associates AU, eBay Partner Network |
| Featured listings | Retailers pay for top placement |
| Premium alerts | $4.99/mo unlimited instant alerts |
| AI subscription | Unlimited AI advisor queries |

**Don't monetize until you have 1,000+ active users.** Focus on retention first.

## ASO (When You Ship Native App)

**App name:** PriceMate Australia — True Price Finder  
**Subtitle:** Coupons, Student Deals & AI Advisor  
**Keywords:** price comparison, student discount, coupon, JB Hi-Fi, Australia deals

## Metrics to Track

- Daily searches
- Student Mode toggle rate
- AI Advisor messages per user
- Coupon copy clicks
- Affiliate link clicks (when live)
- Return visits within 7 days
